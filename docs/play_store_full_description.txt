Exam Ace helps you organise competitive exam preparation in one place — syllabus, daily work, mock tests, and real exam attempts, with a clear view of progress.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Syllabus — Subjects, chapters, topics; mark completion so gaps are visible.
• Daily tasks — Plan work on specific dates; home focuses on today.
• Mock tests — Log scores; link attempts to subjects or topics; charts on subject screens.
• Exams — Record upcoming and completed real exam attempts (separate from mocks).
• Calendar history — Jump to any past date to see what you completed.
• Weekly pulse — Week-at-a-glance consistency (adjust how week % works in Profile).
• Reminders — Optional local reminders on your device.

WHO IT’S FOR
Anyone building a structured plan for competitive exams — you bring the material; Exam Ace helps you track execution and consistency.

INDEPENDENCE & OFFICIAL INFORMATION
Exam Ace is built by an independent developer. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any exam conducting body, commission, or employer. The app is a personal planner only: you enter your own syllabus, tasks, scores, and notes. It does not publish official notifications, syllabi, eligibility rules, admit cards, or results from any authority.

For authentic rules, dates, and results, always rely on official portals — for example:
• https://upsc.gov.in
• https://ssc.gov.in
• https://indianrailways.gov.in
• https://www.india.gov.in
For state-level exams, use your state’s official .gov site or the relevant public service commission.

ACCOUNT & DATA
Sign-in syncs and backs up your data. The app links to a privacy policy and explains how to request data or account deletion. Exam Ace does not show third-party ads in the app.

Good luck with your preparation.
